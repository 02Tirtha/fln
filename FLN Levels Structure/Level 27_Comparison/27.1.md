# Sub-Level 27.1

### **Description**

This level contains the same comparison concepts with reduced complexity. 

Students compare numbers with larger differences and solve structured comparison questions with minimal place value reasoning.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Symbol Selection**

* Fill in \>, \<, or \=.

#### **Section B: Greater and Smaller Number**

* Identify larger and smaller numbers.

#### **Section C: Missing Number Practice**

* Complete simple comparison statements.

### **Examples**

* 25 \_\_ 40  
* 67 \_\_ 67  
* Which is smaller: 81 or 94?  
* \_\_ \< 50  
* 33 \_\_ 41
