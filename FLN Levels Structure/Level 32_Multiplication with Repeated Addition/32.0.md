# Main Level 32.0

### **Description**

This level assesses mastery of multiplication concepts through equal groups, repeated addition, and multiplication facts. 

Students independently determine totals from visual groups and solve multiplication problems involving the 2, 3, and 4 tables.

### **Sections**

#### **Section A: Count Equal Groups**

* Count total objects arranged in equal groups.  
* Determine the total quantity represented by groups.

**Examples**

* 🍎🍎🍎 | 🍎🍎🍎 | 🍎🍎🍎  
* 🐟🐟🐟🐟 | 🐟🐟🐟🐟

#### **Section B: Repeated Addition**

* Find totals using repeated addition.

**Examples**

* 3 \+ 3 \+ 3 \= \_\_\_  
* 4 \+ 4 \+ 4 \= \_\_\_

#### **Section C: Multiplication Facts**

* Solve multiplication facts from tables 2, 3, and 4\.

**Examples**

* 2 × 4 \= \_\_\_  
* 3 × 3 \= \_\_\_  
* 4 × 2 \= \_\_\_  
* 3 × 4 \= \_\_\_
